uCELL Specification

uCELL: 16×16 pixel base unit
Buffer: 2px border around content
Content Area: 12×12 pixels (16 - 2×2)
Font: Monaspace monospace at 12px
Line Height: 16px (matches cell height)
Alignment: Baseline at row 9 of 16
