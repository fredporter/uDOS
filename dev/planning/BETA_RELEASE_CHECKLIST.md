## ⚠️ Risk Mitigation

### Identified Risks

**Technical:**
- **Risk:** Critical bugs in core systems
- **Mitigation:** Extensive beta testing, quick hot-fix process
- **Contingency:** Rollback capability, clear deprecation notices

**Community:**
- **Risk:** Low adoption or engagement
- **Mitigation:** Active outreach, quality content, responsive support
- **Contingency:** Extend beta period, gather feedback, iterate

**Content:**
- **Risk:** Inaccurate or incomplete guides
- **Mitigation:** Peer review, source citations, community validation
- **Contingency:** Clear disclaimers, correction process, version tracking
