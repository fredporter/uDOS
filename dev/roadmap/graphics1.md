	•	Panel blocks that all:
	•	Have even spacing above/below (top/middle/bottom rows),
	•	Are centred with [TITLES] in square brackets,
	•	Have consistent side padding (generated to a fixed width),
	•	A mix of solid, shaded, and progress styles,
	•	No diagonals anywhere.

All examples are ready to drop into a .md file.
