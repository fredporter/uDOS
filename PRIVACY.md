# Privacy Statement

## uDOS Privacy Commitment

The uDOS project is free and open-source software. We respect your privacy and are committed to transparency.

### What uDOS Is

- **Free and Open-Source**: Available for anyone to use, modify, and study
- **Personal Use**: Designed and intended for personal, non-commercial use
- **Privacy-Respecting**: Built with privacy and offline-first principles

### What We Don't Do

- We do not collect personal data
- We do not track usage or analytics
- We do not share information with third parties
- We do not monetize user data

### Credits & Attribution

We credit and acknowledge original developers and contributors whose work has inspired or been incorporated into uDOS. See [CREDITS.md](CREDITS.md) for details.

### Retro Computing Tribute

Parts of uDOS are inspired by retro computing and classic system designs. This is done as a tribute and homage to computing history. We do not intend to infringe on any copyrights, trademarks, or intellectual property rights.

### Use Restrictions

- **Personal Use Only**: uDOS is intended for personal, educational, and non-commercial use
- **Not for Business**: This software is not designed or licensed for commercial or business use without explicit permission

### Your Responsibility

As an open-source project, users are responsible for their own use of the software and any modifications they make.
