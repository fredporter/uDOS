# Welcome to the uDOS wiki!

Updated: 2026-03-05

Wikis provide a place in your repository to lay out the roadmap of your project, show the current status, and document software better, together.

uDOS is a playable, terminal-first runtime where learning the interface and building real output happen at the same time.
This wiki is your first-step public introduction. It shows the surface of each uDOS element.
When you want implementation details and full contracts, jump into `docs/`.
