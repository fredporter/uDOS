## 🎮 **RPG INTEGRATION SYSTEM**

### **Character System (NetHack-inspired)**
- **Six Ability Scores**: STR, INT, DEX, CON, WIS, CHA
- **Progression System**: Level-based with automatic stat increases
- **Inventory Management**: Slot-based equipment tracking
- **Story Integration**: Flags and progression markers
- **Save/Load System**: Persistent character states

### **Object System**
- **Comprehensive Categories**: 8+ item types per theme
- **Enchantment System**: +/- enhancement values
- **Durability Mechanics**: Condition tracking from broken to perfect
- **Special Properties**: Cursed, blessed, magical, artifact items
- **Rarity Levels**: Common to legendary item progression

### **Location/Time Tracking**
- **uDOS Timestamp Format**: `udos-YYMMDD-HHSS-TMZO-MAPTILE-ZOOM`
- **Map Integration**: Coordinate-based location tracking
- **Theme-Specific Systems**: Galactic, laboratory, dungeon coordinates
- **Gameplay Integration**: Story progression and exploration tracking
