## Survival Notes

### Climate Adaptation

London has a mild oceanic climate but weather is highly variable.

**Typical Conditions**:

- **Spring (Mar-May)**: 8-15¬∞C, variable, showers
- **Summer (Jun-Aug)**: 15-25¬∞C, occasional heatwaves
- **Autumn (Sep-Nov)**: 8-15¬∞C, rainy, fog
- **Winter (Dec-Feb)**: 2-8¬∞C, cold rain, rare snow

**Always carry**:

- [ ] Umbrella (compact)
- [ ] Waterproof layer
- [ ] Layers for temperature changes
- [ ] Phone charger

### Weather Wisdom

"There's no such thing as bad weather, only inappropriate clothing."

- Check forecast daily - changes rapidly
- Morning sun doesn't guarantee afternoon sun
- Dress in layers

### Hazards

| Hazard | Season | Mitigation |
| ------ | ------ | ---------- |
| Cold/wet exposure | Autumn-Spring | Waterproof layers |
| Thames flooding | Tidal | Stay back from riverbank |
| Pickpockets | Year-round | Secure valuables |
| Cycling traffic | Year-round | Look both ways |

### Water Sources

- **Tap water**: Excellent quality, safe
- **Public fountains**: Limited, parks
- **Free water refills**: Many cafes, ask nicely

### Shelter

- **Tube stations**: Warm, dry, extensive
- **Public libraries**: Free, Wi-Fi
- **Shopping centres**: Westfield, etc.
- **Churches**: Often open during day

## Transport

### The Tube (Underground)

World's oldest metro system, 11 lines, 270+ stations.

| Line | Colour | Key Stops |
| ---- | ------ | --------- |
| Central | Red | Oxford Circus, Liverpool St |
| Northern | Black | King's Cross, Bank |
| Victoria | Light Blue | Victoria, King's Cross |
| Piccadilly | Dark Blue | Heathrow, Leicester Sq |
| District | Green | Westminster, Victoria |
| Circle | Yellow | All central stations |

### Oyster Card / Contactless

**Oyster Card** or **Contactless bank card** for:

- Tube
- Buses
- DLR
- Overground
- Some National Rail

Daily cap ensures you never overpay.

### Key Stations

- **King's Cross St Pancras** - Eurostar, major hub
- **Victoria** - Gatwick trains, coaches
- **Paddington** - Heathrow Express
- **Liverpool Street** - Stansted, east
- **Waterloo** - Southern routes

### Tips

- Stand on the right on escalators
- "Mind the Gap" - platform gaps real
- Night Tube runs Fri/Sat on some lines
- Bus is often better for short trips

## Local Knowledge

### Queuing

**The Queue is Sacred**. Always join at the back. Never push in.

### Pubs

- Last orders typically 11pm
- Food served until ~9pm
- No table service - order at bar
- Tip not expected but appreciated

### Free Museums

Major museums free to enter:

- British Museum
- Natural History Museum
- Science Museum
- V&A
- Tate Modern
- National Gallery

### Tipping

- Restaurants: 10-12.5% if not included
- Taxis: Round up
- Pubs: Not expected
