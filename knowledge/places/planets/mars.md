## Mars Environment

### Atmosphere

| Gas | Percentage |
| --- | ---------- |
| Carbon Dioxide | 95.3% |
| Nitrogen | 2.7% |
| Argon | 1.6% |
| Oxygen | 0.13% |
| Carbon Monoxide | 0.08% |

Surface pressure is ~0.6% of Earth - water boils at body temperature.

### Dust Storms

- Local dust devils common
- Regional storms seasonal
- Global storms rare but planet-covering
- Can last months

### Radiation

No magnetic field or thick atmosphere = high radiation:

- Surface radiation ~0.67 mSv/day
- Earth surface ~0.01 mSv/day
- Major challenge for human missions

### Seasons

Mars has similar axial tilt to Earth (25.2¬∞ vs 23.4¬∞), creating seasons:

- Northern summer: Longer, cooler (Mars farther from Sun)
- Southern summer: Shorter, warmer
