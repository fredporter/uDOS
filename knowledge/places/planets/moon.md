## Lunar Environment

### Surface Conditions

| Factor | Condition |
| ------ | --------- |
| Vacuum | No atmosphere |
| Temperature Day | +127¬∞C |
| Temperature Night | -173¬∞C |
| Day/Night Cycle | ~14 Earth days each |
| Radiation | High (no magnetic field) |
| Dust | Fine, abrasive, clingy |

### Lunar Regolith

Fine dust covering surface:

- Depth: 2-8 meters
- Grain size: 40-130 micrometers
- Composition: Silicates, glass beads
- Hazard: Abrasive, lung-damaging

### Water Ice

Confirmed in permanently shadowed craters:

- South Pole: Shackleton, other craters
- North Pole: Similar deposits
- Source: Comet impacts, solar wind

Essential for future bases - drinking water, oxygen, rocket fuel.

### Lunar Libration

Moon appears to "wobble" slightly, allowing us to see ~59% of surface over time (not just 50%).

## Future Exploration

### Artemis Program (NASA)

Goal: Return humans to Moon by mid-2020s

| Mission | Target |
| ------- | ------ |
| Artemis I | Uncrewed orbital (completed 2022) |
| Artemis II | Crewed orbital (planned 2025) |
| Artemis III | First landing since 1972 |
| Gateway | Lunar orbital station |

Landing site: South Pole region (water ice access)

### Commercial Missions

- SpaceX: Starship HLS (Human Landing System)
- Blue Origin: Blue Moon lander
- Astrobotic, Intuitive Machines: Cargo landers

### Resources

| Resource | Location | Use |
