2. CORRECT DOSAGE/METHOD:              
   Follow instructions precisely for any  
   chemicals (e.g., bleach, iodine) or

   Allow required contact time for chemicals
   or boiling. Rushing can compromise safety.

   Filter heavily turbid water first.     
   Purification methods have limits.
