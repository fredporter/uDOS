## âš ï¸ Critical Safety Rules

**Before Starting:**
- [ ] Check local fire restrictions/bans
- [ ] Clear 3m diameter to mineral soil
- [ ] Water or sand for extinguishing nearby
- [ ] Know wind direction and speed
- [ ] Identify fire escape route

**During Fire:**
- [ ] Never leave unattended
- [ ] Keep fire size appropriate (cooking/warmth only)
- [ ] Monitor for sparks/embers spreading
- [ ] Maintain control at all times
- [ ] Keep children/pets at safe distance

**After Fire:**
- [ ] Drown with water (not bury with dirt first)
- [ ] Stir and soak again
- [ ] Hand test for heat (all areas cool)
- [ ] Scatter cold ashes
- [ ] Check area before leaving

**Wildfire Risk:** If conditions are hot, dry, and windy - DO NOT start a fire. Use stove or eat cold food.
