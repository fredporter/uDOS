# Campfire Activities & Morale Games

**Category:** Skills — Morale & Group Cohesion
**Difficulty:** Beginner
**Time:** 5-30 minutes

---

## Overview

Morale is a survival resource. Simple, low-tech games keep spirits up and sharpen awareness.

---

## Game 1: Word Chain

**Goal:** Build a chain of words where each starts with the last letter of the previous word.

Example:
```
River → Rock → Kite → Echo → Oak
```

---

## Game 2: Story Prompt Relay

**Goal:** Each person adds one sentence to build a story.

Prompt ideas:
- "We found a map etched into stone..."
- "The radio crackled at midnight..."
- "A storm rolled in faster than expected..."

---

## Game 3: Observation Round

**Goal:** Test short-term memory and attention.

1. One person arranges 8-12 small items.
2. Everyone studies for 30 seconds.
3. Items are covered, then recalled.

---

## Notes

- Keep sessions short and rotating to prevent fatigue.
- Use games to rehearse decision-making and teamwork.
