# Bushfire Survival Guide

**Category:** Emergency Response | Priority: Critical
**Season:** High Risk during hot, dry months (typically summer)
**Relevance:** Australian conditions (eucalyptus forests, high fire risk)

---

## Overview

Bushfires are one of the deadliest natural hazards in fire-prone regions. This guide covers survival strategies for Australian conditions, vegetation, and fire behavior - but principles apply to wildfire situations worldwide.

**High-Risk Regions** (examples in Australia):
- Forested mountain ranges near urban areas
- Areas with dense eucalyptus vegetation
- Regions with hot, dry summers and strong winds
- Coastal forests with dry undergrowth
- Remote bush areas with limited road access
