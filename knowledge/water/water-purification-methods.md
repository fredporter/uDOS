### Water Purification Method Selection

```flow
st=>start: Need safe water
fire=>condition: Can make fire?
chem=>condition: Have chemicals?
uv=>condition: Have UV device?
filter=>condition: Have filter?
boil=>operation: Boil water
chemical=>operation: Chemical treatment
uvlight=>operation: UV purification
filtration=>operation: Filter water
distill=>operation: Solar distillation
safe=>end: Safe to drink

st->fire
fire(yes)->boil->safe
fire(no)->chem
chem(yes)->chemical->safe
chem(no)->uv
uv(yes)->uvlight->safe
uv(no)->filter
filter(yes)->filtration->safe
filter(no)->distill->safe
```

### Boiling Purification Sequence

```sequence
Start->Water: Collect water source
Water->Filter: Strain through cloth
Filter->Container: Pour into pot
Container->Heat: Place on fire/stove
Heat->Boil: Bring to rolling boil
Boil->Timer: Boil 1-10 min based on elevation
Timer->Cool: Remove from heat
Cool->Store: Transfer to clean container
Store->Safe: Water ready to drink
```
