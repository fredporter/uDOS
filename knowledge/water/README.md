# Water Procurement & Purification

Finding, purifying, and storing water safely.

## Contents

- **Finding Water**: Natural sources, indicators, collection
- **Purification Methods**: Boiling, filtering, chemical, UV
- **Storage**: Safe containers, preventing contamination
- **Conservation**: Using water efficiently
- **Well Construction**: Digging, lining, maintenance
- **Rainwater Harvesting**: Collection systems

## Critical Priority

Water is a top survival priority:
- **Rule of 3s**: 3 days without water (average)
- **Daily Needs**: 2-3 liters minimum per person
- **Purification Required**: Assume all natural water is contaminated

## Purification Methods

1. **Boiling**: 1 minute at rolling boil (3 min at altitude)
2. **Filtration**: Ceramic, sand, charcoal filters
3. **Chemical**: Chlorine, iodine tablets
4. **UV**: Sunlight (SODIS method)
5. **Distillation**: Solar stills, evaporation
