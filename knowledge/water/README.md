**Category:** Water | **Target:** 150 guides | **Current:** 26 guides (17.3%)

Finding, purifying, and storing water safely in all environments.

---

## Critical Priority

Water is a top survival priority:
- **Rule of 3s**: 3 days without water (average)
- **Daily Needs**: 2-3 liters minimum per person
- **Purification Required**: Assume all natural water is contaminated

## Purification Methods Reference

1. **Boiling**: 1 minute at rolling boil (3 min at altitude)
2. **Filtration**: Ceramic, sand, charcoal filters
3. **Chemical**: Chlorine, iodine tablets
4. **UV**: Sunlight (SODIS method)
5. **Distillation**: Solar stills, evaporation
